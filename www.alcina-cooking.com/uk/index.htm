My name is Alcina Mazers and I live in Allauch, just a stone's throw from Marseille.
Cooking is my life's passion and I have been fortunate enough to gain practical, professional experience from some of the leading chefs in our region and further afield.

As a result, I now offer cooking lessons right in the comfort of your own home in the Bouches du Rhône and Var regions.

Lessons can be held just a few hours before dinner time, so that everything is ready when your guests arrive without the slightest hitch.

Alternatively, lessons can be given at any other time to suit your requirements.
I also run workshops for both adults and children, an ideal opportunity for having your best friends round. You can choose the theme for the workshop, ranging from everyday basic cooking to the most imaginative creations, worthy of renowned master chefs, to amaze your friends and family.
