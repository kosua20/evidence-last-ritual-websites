Greetings, and Welcome my Website Devoted to
Complementary and Alternative Medicines.

Alternative medicines comprise therapies based upon:
- the value of plants (herbatherapy, aromatherapy),
- the manipulation of limbs or energies (kinesiology, reflexology, radiesthesy),
- or the principals of mental, emotional or spiritual work.


Far from the pharmaceutical industry lobby's party line repeated by a press muzzled by political and economic interests, I wanted to offer on my site an alternative view of therapies that may help cure us of certain troubles or illnesses.

I am neither a doctor nor a practitioner, but an everyday person interested in simple and natural methods for easing certain forms of suffering and increasing our feeling of wellbeing. I hope that reading these pages will open new horizons and inspire you to document or test for yourself some of theses practices.

Any and all suggestions for improving this site are welcome. Feel free to contact me as well if you wish to make a correction, provide additional information or leave a testimonial.

Please note that neither serious illnesses (such as cancer) nor infectious illnesses fall within the scope of alternative medicines.
As a general rule, always consult a licensed physician or practitioner.
