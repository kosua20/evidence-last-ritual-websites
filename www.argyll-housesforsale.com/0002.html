17 Mossfield Drive (Oban)

Price: £140,000

Mossfield Drive is located just off the Glencruitten Road, in a quiet, sought-after residential area. The property enjoys views of the hills of Soroba to the front and the cliff face flanking the Glencruitten Road to the rear.

Within walking distance from Oban, and amongst an established community, this property offers a fine opportunity for the successful purchaser.

Oban is an expanding port with an attractive harbour. It has a full range of shopping, recreational facilities and professional offices as well as a number of primary schools, a High School and a hospital. Mossfield Drive is also convenient for the 18-hole golf course at Glencruitten and for exploring the surrounding countryside.

Drive along George Street, through the town centre to Argyll Square, along Combie Street to the Parish Church. Fork left and continue up Glencruitten Road. Take the second right into Mossfield Drive, and bear round to the left. Number 17 can be found on the left hand side.

No 17 has recently been modernised and upgraded throughout, resulting in a beautifully presented, well appointed home. From the entrance hallway, access is gained into the spacious living room, with a walk-in, under stair cubby, fitted out for a pc. The rear lobby provides access to the downstairs toilet, storage cupboard, rear garden and the kitchen. From the entrance hall, the wide staircase rises to the upper landing with a clean, modern bathroom, and two well proportioned bedrooms. One of the features of this property is the wooden finishes and neutral décor throughout. The lighting is a mixture of pendant lights, ceiling lights and modern spotlights, and there is a neutral carpet throughout, with vinyl floor covering in the kitchen and rear lobby areas and in the bathroom.

The property also benefits from a mono block paved driveway, with wrought iron parking gates and a sizeable, mature rear garden.
