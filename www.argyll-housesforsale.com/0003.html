1 Lower Achagoyle (Oban)

Price: £110,000

Lower Achagoyle is an attractive terrace situated alongside the A83 Oban/Lochgilphead road at Minard occupying an elevated position overlooking Loch Fyne and the surrounding hills. No. 1 is situated at the end of the terrace and it does not directly face Loch Fyne. However, it still has good views over the loch and enjoys more privacy from its location on the side of the building.

The property is close to the local village amenities including a post office/shop within the terrace. The village also supports a church and a primary school – with a hotel at nearby Lochgair and a pub at the neighbouring village of Furnace. A wider range of facilities, including shops, professional services, High School and a Hospital, can be found at Lochgilphead approximately 12 miles away. The Glasgow – Campbeltown bus service runs 3 times daily and there is also a school bus service in operation to the High School.

Lower Achagoyle has a great deal of charm and is thought to date from the mid 19th century, being former forestry cottages. No. 1 has been very well maintained and up-graded by the current owners and is in an excellent state of decoration.

The accommodation is on two floors and has attractive dormer windows to the front, making two nicely shaped bedrooms upstairs. Downstairs there is a living room, kitchen – with room for a small table and chairs – and a bathroom.

The property is double-glazed and benefits from Total Control electric storage heating.

The cottage has its own private area of garden and ample parking. It also has a designated store within a well maintained row of stone outbuildings situated at the back of the terrace where the neighbouring gardens are all nicely tended.

It is equally suitable as a permanent family home or as a second home/holiday base. It would also be ideal for holiday letting particularly as the area is a popular holiday base.
