Raslie, Glenshellach Road (Oban)

Price: £170,000

The property is a pleasant walk from the town centre where there is a wide range of shopping and leisure facilities. In addition, Oban has a number of primary schools, a high school and a modern doctors’ surgery. There are regular daily trains to and from Glasgow and Oban is an expanding port with an attractive sea front and busy harbour from which the Caledonian MacBrayne ferries operate serving the Inner Hebrides and some of the Outer Hebrides.

Raslie is a substantial detached property with a sunny south facing aspect. It has been well maintained and upgraded and offers excellent family accommodation over two floors. Benefiting from modern décor and finishes, the accommodation comprises: vestibule, spacious hall, living room, dining kitchen, three bedrooms, bathroom and downstairs shower room. An external lean-to extension at the rear provides a useful utility area and store.

The property benefits from oil-fired central heating and is double glazed.

There is a small and easily maintained area of garden ground and a hedge to the front offering privacy from the public road.
