Situation
Ardconnel Villa is a prestigious and much admired property situated in a sought after residential area, on Oban Hill. It enjoys a superb open aspect with panoramic views from its vantage point at the corner of Rockfield Road and Ardconnel Terrace.

The expansive views from the front range from McCaigs Tower and down Ardconnel Terrace to the Sound of Mull, Oban Bay, the South Pier and the ferry terminal. The side windows also offer good views and look down Rockfield Road to the South Pier, Pulpit Hill and the hills of Glenshellach.

The property is a short walking distance from the town centre where there is a wide range of shopping and leisure facilities. Oban also has a number of primary schools, a high school, a medical centre, a modern hospital and churches of various denominations.

Oban is an expanding port with an attractive sea front and busy harbour from which the Caledonian MacBrayne ferries operate serving the Inner Hebrides and some of the Outer Hebrides.

Follow the road around the sharp left hand bend and continue climbing until you see the property, situated on the corner, on the right hand side of the road.

Description
Ardconnel Villa is a substantial Victorian villa comprising four apartments. Number 2 is on the upper floor and benefits from a great deal of natural light, in particular from the large bay window in the lounge which also enjoys superb sunsets.

The shared entrance door, situated to the front of the property, opens to an impressive reception hall, with the original staircase, lit by a stained glass window on the half-turn, leading to the first floor landing and access to the apartment.

The property is bright and airy and benefits from high ceilings, with the original cornice work and ceiling roses a particular feature. It also benefits from mains gas central heating and has secondary glazing. It has stripped pine doors, matching surrounds and skirting boards and is very nicely presented – with tasteful décor and a lovely, professionally designed, kitchen.

Outside, there is a small area of garden to the front and a large shared garden and drying green at the rear of the building.
