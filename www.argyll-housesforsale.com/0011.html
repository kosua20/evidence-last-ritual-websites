13 Jura Road, Soroba (Oban)

Price: £98,000

Situation
This mid-terraced dwelling house is situated within the Soroba housing development and is convenient for the town centre, where there is a full range of shopping and recreational facilities. There is a regular bus service in operation.

It is well positioned in a terrace of established houses and enjoys a very bright and sunny outlook over playing fields and the hills at Glenshellach.

Directions
Heading south out of Oban on the A816 – turn left into McCaig Road and continue to the Soroba housing development, turning left at the telephone box into Jura Road. Number 13 is situated on the left hand side of the road.

Description
The house is very tastefully presented, with attractive décor, and has a new carpet in the lounge and laminate flooring in the entrance hall. It offers airy accommodation ideal for a family and comprises: spacious hall, through lounge with ample room for dining, kitchen, three bedrooms, and a bathroom. It benefits from double glazed windows and has recently had a gas central heating system installed.

The property will undoubtedly appeal to the first time buyer or perhaps to a couple with a young family. Primary schools and the secondary school are located nearby and there are play areas within the development, which has a good local community spirit.
