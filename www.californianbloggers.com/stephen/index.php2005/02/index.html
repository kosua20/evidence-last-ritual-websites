Monday 14 February 2005

f*****g valentine

By Stephen, Monday 14 February 2005 at 23:58 :: posted to General

“Valentine's Day Feb 14th: Spread the Love Around!”

This is what appeared in my mailbox when I came back from work today. And right after that my mum called and the first thing she asked was: “ well, where will you be dining out on Valentine’s day, honey?”

Why do my parents always torture me?

I wanted to say “nowhere mum ‘cause you’re son is a looser… and by the way, stop calling me”… but I just answered “I haven’t made plans yet, luv u mum, bye”.

Some days I feel like I’m still 15.

music of the day: Serge Gainsbourg
