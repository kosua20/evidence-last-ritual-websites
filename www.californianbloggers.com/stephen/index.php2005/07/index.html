Wednesday 6 July 2005

Philly

By Stephen, Wednesday 6 July 2005 at 15:59 :: posted to General

Heya, my stay in Philly and NYC kicked ass. I went to the coolest record store ever. It is called The Marvelous and you find there everything you want: graphic novels, zines, vinyls, new and used CDs…

I heard that the Marvelous features live performances by bands like Cheese on Bread and Cordalene, and that they will soon start hosting a monthly night of live electronic music at Millcreek.

I like this place. I am pissed to miss their anniversary party at the end of the month.



There was also this girl, Mirah, who I met at a party in NYC. We talked a lot, she’s super smart and super cute. She told me she may visit sometimes. I hope she does.
