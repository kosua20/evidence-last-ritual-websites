Wednesday 14 September 2005

Mirahaha

By Stephen, Wednesday 14 September 2005 at 11:33 :: posted to General

Mirah came for one day last week. She had some kind of job interview in LA. We went to a club where I’m Djing… it was so much fun. She helped me pick music up. All my friends were there too. I was spinning my tunes and everybody was dancing … awesome.
Then we went back home and talked all night long. It’s weird how well we get along. She understands my fear of falling under any kind of mental domination, my passion for music, my desire to quit my job. And I think I understand what she’s been through in her childhood and the present repercussions and consequences. It’s a shame we don’t live closer to each other… it is really frustrating or maybe that’s what makes it so intense.
