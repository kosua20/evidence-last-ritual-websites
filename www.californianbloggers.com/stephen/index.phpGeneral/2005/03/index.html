Wednesday 30 March 2005

trains

By Stephen, Wednesday 30 March 2005 at 23:06 :: posted to General

I don’t have a car, can you believe that? I do. I’m riding my bike… and it’s the best way I have found to take a stand against pollution.

The only problem is when you don’t have a car you cannot easily visit friends or go to shows out of town. Wouldn’t it be great to be able to hop trains and to go wherever we want? We would all be hobos hitching free rides from place to place… and it would be a good way to meet new friends too.



I took this shot a long time ago, I think it was in 2000. It was at Bloomington, IN for the Bloomington fest. There were great bands there: early day miners, low, casket lottery, cursive, bevel, panoply academy-legionnaires, Julie Doiron, Shannon Wright…

I should go back to B-town, I like this place a lot. I need to take some days off… this is an EMERGENCY.
