Firstly, a selection of sites for the specialists of the past using the tools of the future :

http://gallica.bnf.fr The web site of the French National Library allows you to download old works.

http://www.sudoc.abes.fr/ The complete catalogue of all the works in French university libraries.

http://rafale.worldnet.net/~atluigi/info/sommaire.html An excellent site on esoterism, alchemy and magic, including an interesting number of resources.

http://www.louvre.fr/ This one needs no introduction - the web site of the Louvre!

http://ndparis.free.fr/ Some interesting and well-illustrated pages on the Notre Dame of Paris

http://www.citadelle.org/ Citadel is completely dedicated to the Middle Ages and comes complete with a collection of unusual articles and a documentary database. Have a browse through the forum, where you might end up being called an inquisitor if you have the misfortune to upset one of its hosts! Obviously an historian's sense of humour :o)

http://www.salemwitchmystery.com Kristin Lark's site (a good American friend of mine) about the Salem witch trials
