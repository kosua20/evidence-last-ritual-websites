The private detective: an essential course of action!

A private detective's mission involves collecting information intended for third parties for the purpose of defending their interests, without divulging his/her occupation nor revealing the purpose of his/her mission (Art. 20 of Act no. 83—629 of 12 July 1983).

 

To discover the history and rules behind our profession, refer to the Profession section, which describes all the guarantees to which you will be entitled when calling on our services.
