Italian language and literature classes:

Three different classes are on offer, which you can join at any time of the year:

  • Italian for beginners
  • Italian for intermediates
  • Italian literature

All classes are taught by qualified teachers as part of the partnerships signed by the "Dante Circle of Friends" association with the Spanish Ministry of Education.

Italian cookery classes:

Every Wednesday and Saturday, we are offering Italian cookery classes (duration: 2 hr / class).

Classes are run by chef Luigi Bruno, owner of the "O Sole Mio" pizzeria in Granada and a benefactor of the "Dante Circle of Friends" association.

For details on how to enrol, class times and costs: CONTACT

Trips to Italy:

Every month, we offer a selection of trips to Italy for which members of the "Dante Circle of Friends" association can take advantage of very attractive special prices. Such prices can only be offered thanks to the partnerships that the "Dante Circle of Friends" association has signed with the leading Spanish tour operators. You can choose from different packages:

  • Cultural weekend
  • Organised tours with conferences and activities
  • Open trip lasting one or two weeks

For those members enrolled in the Italian classes, we organise two cultural trips every year in the Castelraimondo region. During the trips, the following activities are on offer:

  • Visits to Italian cultural sites
  • Conferences with historians and specialists in Italian art
  • Meetings with local artists
  • Participation in shows or local events (according to the period)
  • Italian classes with real-life situations

These two trips are organised as part of cultural exchanges between Spain and Italy, with the partnership of the Spanish Ministry for the Arts.
