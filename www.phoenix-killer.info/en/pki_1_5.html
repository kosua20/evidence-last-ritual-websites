The ICPA is a private association created by friends and colleagues of journalist Jack Lorski in the wake of his murder in Scotland. The ICPA is aimed at helping international police and law departments to bring a swift end to the Phoenix's killing spree.

  • SKL-NETWORK.COM

SKL NETWORK is the agency that Jack Lorski worked for. It decided to go public with the two CD-ROMs received from the Phoenix.

  • LIBERATION.FR

LIBERATION.FR has published several articles on the Phoenix case. Use its search engine to access its archives.

TIMELINE

11-1945  DISCOVERY OF THE NAG HAMMADI TEXTS
An Egyptian peasant discovers jars containing several religious and philosophical texts written between the 1st and 4th centuries AD. Officially, only thirteen codices have been found (including Codex 2 containing the famous "Gospel of Thomas"), but a mysterious Codex 14 seems to have escaped any census.

08-1975  DISAPPEARANCE OF PETER VOLKER
Peter Volker, a historian of religions, mysteriously disappears while visiting Rhodes for participate in a conference on Meroitic (an ancient language spoken at the time of Christ).

04-2001  DISCOVERY OF A SUPER 8 FILM
Jack Lorski, an investigative journalist at SKL NETWORK, finds an amateur film, shot in 1975 by Karen Gijman's father, which proves the murder of Peter Volker by professional killers.

07-2001  AN ARTICLE ON THE VOLKER CASE IS PUBLISHED
After three months of investigation in Rhodes and Istanbul, Jack Lorski reveals that Peter Volker was murdered by a mysterious organization tasked with recovering Codex 14, whose professor had purchased it in 1973.

11-2001  DEATH OF GIANNI MAGGIOLI
The body of Gianni Maggioli, son of an Italian industrialist, was found in Naples, near the Dominican convent of San Domenico di Maggiore. He had been shot in the head.

12-2001  DEATH OF BROTHER FRANCESCO
The body of Brother Francesco, a Dominican priest and librarian, was found in Genoa, in the cellars of the convent of Santa Maria di Castello. His throat was slit.

01-2002  DEATH OF ABBÉ MALARD
The body of Abbé Malard, founder of the Order of the Scouts of Saint Paul, is found in Geneva, very close to the University. He was held captive and tortured for a week before succumbing to cardiac arrest.

02-2002  DEATH OF JOSÉ CORTADO
The body of José Cortado, journalist for Impact magazine, is found in Toulouse, in front of the House of the Inquisition. He was drained of blood.

03-2002  DEATH OF HELENA WHITFORD
The body of Helena Whitford, a theology student, is found in Oxford, in the cellars of Balliol College. She was strangled.

03-2002  JACK LORSKI'S INVESTIGATION IN ENGLAND
Due to evidence consistent with the Volker case, Jack Lorski goes to Oxford to investigate the circumstances of Helena Whitford's murder.

05-2002  JACK LORSKI'S INVESTIGATION IN ITALY
In Rome, Jack Lorski meets journalist Antonio Foscarini, who informs him of the murders in Naples, Genoa, Geneva, and Toulouse. Furthermore, he learns that Helena Whitford was a member of Manus Domini, a highly secretive cult organization.

10-2002  DEATH OF ANTONIO FOSCARINI
Thanks to an email signed "The Phoenix," the body of Antonio Foscarini is found by Jack Lorski in the outbuildings of a former disused abbey, east of Paris. He had been burned alive several days earlier.

October 2002  DEATH OF HILDE GARDENER
The body of Hilde Gardener, an art history student, was found in Marburg, in the cellar of a building under construction near St. Elisabeth Church. She had been strangled.

October 2002  DEATH OF CARL BREITNER
The body of Carl Breitner, a theology professor, was found in Wittenberg, in the cellar of a house near the Collegium Augusteum. He was strangled.

12-2002  JACK LORSKI'S INVESTIGATION IN THE CZECH REPUBLIC
Jack Lorski discovers that the locations of the Phoenix murders correspond to the steps taken four centuries earlier by Giordano Bruno, an Italian Renaissance philosopher. To anticipate the next murder, he continues his investigation in Prague.

11-2002  DEATH OF CAROLINA MOLIKOVA
The body of Carolina Molikova, a Czech television employee, is found in the cellars of Benatky Castle,about thirty kilometers from Prague. She was stabbed.

12-2002  DEATH OF MARKUS HERMANN
The body of Markus Hermann, founder of the Intransigent Church in Germany, is found in Frankfurt, a stone's throw from the City and University Library. He has been strangled.

01-2003  KIDNAPPING OF JACK LORSKI AND KAREN GIJMAN
Still trying to prevent another Phoenix murder, Jack Lorski and Karen Gijman travel to Venice. They disappear two days after their arrival.

01-2003  DEATH OF FRANCESCA BARTOLINI
The body of Francesca Bartolini, a cultural organizer, is found in Venice, in the cellars of the Mocenigo Palace. She has been strangled.

02-2003  DEATH OF GUIDO CORLIANO
The body of Guido Corliano, head of Manus Domini in Europe, is found on Rome's Campo Dei Fiori, the same place where Giordano Bruno was burned alive four centuries earlier. He was tortured and then stabbed.

02-2003  APPOINTMENT OF GERD HANKE
After investigating the three Phoenix murders committed in Germany, German police officer Gerd Hanke, a specialist in serial killers, is appointed to coordinate the investigation at the European level.

07-2003  INVESTIGATION BY ALEX BORGO IN CZECH REPUBLIC
Having learned of Carolina Molikova's death but doubting her membership in Manus Domini, Alex Borgo, a freelance journalist, decides to investigate this mysterious secret society and the circumstances of his ex-partner.

10-2003  RELEASE OF THE BLACK PHOENIX CD-ROM
Arnaud Ivan, President of SKL NETWORK, first receives a video of Karen Gijman, weakened and terrified, as well as a threatening letter signed "The Phoenix" then, a few days later, he receives a black CD-ROM, designed like a video game, which the killer calls his "work". To increase the chances of decrypting this document and finding clues about its author, SKL NETWORK, in agreement with the police, is releasing it to the general public.

02-2004  RELEASE OF JACK LORSKI AND KAREN GIJMAN
Thanks to the decryption of the black CD-ROM, Jack Lorski and Karen Gijman are freed, unharmed, but the Phoenix has vanished. The hostages were being held in an abandoned factory on the outskirts of Milan.

03-2004  DEATH OF ALEX BORGO
Alex Borgo's body is found at Champigny, in his home. He died from a gunshot fired at point-blank range. The police concluded it was suicide.

10-2004  RELEASE OF THE WHITE CD-ROM FROM THE PHOENIX
Arnaud Ivan receives a white CD-ROM from the Phoenix. It contains films shot by Alex Borgo. After authenticating its author, SKL NETWORK and Gerd Hanke decide to release it to the general public. His decryption will prove that Alex Borgo was killed by the Phoenix.

04-2006  JACK LORSKI'S INVESTIGATION IN SPAIN
Jack goes to Andalusia to film the investigation of Manuela Ortiz, a young Malaga police inspector, confronted with the murders of a serial killer.

07-2006  DEATH OF JACK LORSKI
The remains of Jack Lorski's dismembered body are found in Scotland, in Loch Fyne, near Inveraray. The police favor the hypothesis of a new crime involving the Phoenix.

07-2006  CREATION OF THE ICPA
SKL NETWORK journalists, friends of Jack Lorski, create the International Committee for the Arrest of the Phoenix (ICPA) to help the police neutralize the Phoenix.
