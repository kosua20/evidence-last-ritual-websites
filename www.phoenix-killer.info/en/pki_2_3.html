MESSAGES FROM THE PHOENIX

The Phoenix needs to see his power recognized, even going so far as to taunt the police by providing them with clues (the CD-ROMs), as if drawing additional strength from the devices put in place to capture him. This thirst for recognition also translates into his need to communicate with those hunting him and explain his actions, or even justify them. To help you better understand the personality of this killer, we have selected his main messages below.

  • Email sent after the release of Jack Lorski and Karen Gijman

    Everything was planned from the start. Everything.
    Except Jack's stubbornness.
    His obstinacy in following in my footsteps exasperated me at first.
    But very quickly I understood that he and his girlfriend could serve the realization of the Work, at least contribute to making it known.
    Because these two weren't the Chosen Ones.

    Everything was planned. Everything.
    I knew that one day or another someone would manage to decipher the work.
    If it hadn't been you, it would have been someone else.
    I knew that one day or another, they would come to free them.
    They came but didn't find me.
    By the time they arrived to free them, I was already far away. Very far away.
    They won't find me. Never.
    His Work accomplished, the Phoenix flew towards the sun.

    The black Sun, invincible.
