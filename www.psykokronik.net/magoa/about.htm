::ABOUT::

The purpose of MAGOA is quite simple: to reunite in a single website all the best artists in the graphic and collages field. I first started doing collages when I was a big fan of The X-Files, but since the 6th season I've stopped watching it and also stopped doing fanart oriented collages. Its a reason why even if your art isn't related to a tv show you are still accepted, because diversity is always best.

I do not make any money out of this organisation, just as all the other members of MAGOA. All images are belonging to their respective owners. Please do not steal anything from the artists listed here.

FAQ

01. Is Magoa a directory/Fan Listing or something?
Magoa is none of the above, this site leans more to listing potential great web artists who are talented, and take graphic design with a passion. In a way it kind of look like a listing of some sort, but it's a circle of excellent artist who produces creative arts.

02. How do I join Magoa and what does it take?
By applying using the join form. It takes lots of creativity, at least some good artworks and a little of your time.

03. What do I do once my site's approved?
I can only say "congratulations" you're accepted into Magoa, all you need to do now is to place a text link or button up on your site, on anywhere visible. If you don't, then no matter how "out of this world" your art is, I will not list you down.

04. I've been rejected :( what now?
Don't feel dejected, it does not mean the end of the world. You are allowed to re-apply again. Once you're brimming with confidence, go ahead the join form is always open.

05. Is Magoa Elite?
Magoa only list the best fanart site on the net(at least the ones that have been submitted), it is no way elite in any shape or form. Anyone is free to join, just bear in mind, there will always be a chance that your site might get rejected, if your arts is not up to our standard or expectation.
