 TESTIMONIALS

"My blood ran cold when I came across the package on October 2. I immediately had a bad feeling about it. Personally, I was convinced that it was the handiwork of the Phoenix. The signs were unmistakable. Without wasting any time, I warned my associates as well as Jack Lorski, who instantly warned me to notify the police, so that the envelope and its contents could be analyzed. I have no idea what the Phoenix is looking to achieve by sending us a new CD-ROM, but I am sure that he has made a mistake in breaking cover".

Arnaud Ivan, CEO of SKL Network
 
"When Arnaud told me what he had discovered, my first impression was one of surprise that the Phoenix was back on the scene. When I was hot on his trail, the murder in Paris convinced me that he was following some sort of ritualized revenge that would end with his 12th victim. This new turn of events has me worried. I cannot wait to find out the contents of the new CD-ROM. It might give us an inkling of his intentions, but personally speaking, I think that it can only be a bad omen".
Jack Lorski
 
"I fail to understand how my son's films ended up on the disc. I have every confidence in the police to get to the bottom of this mystery. Whatever happens, it will not bring my son back. If it can prevent other innocent victims from being killed by this madman, then obviously I will agree to my son's films being distributed".
Mrs. Borgo, Alex's mother
