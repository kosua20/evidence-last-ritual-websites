PRESENTATION
 

IKL NETWORK - founded in 1962 by three professional journalists, Arnaud Ivan, Stéphane Karlsberg and Marie Liloux - creates ready-to-use informational content for TV (terrestrial, cable and satellite) and the Internet.

We make, produce, and distribute a complete range of documents with high editorial value, including reports, investigations, magazines, dossiers and city guides.

With the advent of broadband Internet, IKL NETWORK has set up a dedicated "new technologies" cell to provide these reports in the format best suited to their end use: VHS, CD-ROM, Real Video or QuickTime.

 
SKL NETWORK in brief:
 Company with a capital of 75,000 €
 Founded: April 1962

 Personnel: 53 permanent staff and approx. one hundred freelance journalists

HISTORY

1962: Birth of the IKL Agency, specialising in news features

1970: Creation of the photography department

1976: IKL branches out into video reporting

1981: The Digital section is founded

1985: IKL Agency wins a Golden Report award for the feature "The Curse of Tutankhamen" which secured the company's reputation

1989: An international office opens, based in New York City

1990: Creation of the "Web Report" section, specialising in online content

1991: IKL migrates to digital video and offers its features to webTV

1995: IKL Agency becomes SKL Network and launches its own Internet portal

1999: IKL Network goes international big-time, with subsidiaries all over Europe

2003: IKL Network becomes SKL Network and opens its first agency in Asia.
