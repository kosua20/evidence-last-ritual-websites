SECURITY TRAINING CENTER

 


 

SNST is an approved security training center. Our mission is to offer a wide variety of training services enabling our customers to improve their safety. At SNST our students develop skills required for the protection of lives, rights and property.

 

SNST’s certified instructors deliver courses on law enforcement, crisis management, surveillance, self-defense, firearms and driving to help students identify, control or prevent potential threats.

 

Our clients include security operators - local, state, and federal law enforcement agencies - multi-national corporations and non-governmental organizations.
