TRAINING

 

Firearms training (FT)

The training incorporates courses to develop shooting skills in a variety of realistic attack situations (speed shooting, multiple shooting, multiple targets, shooting while moving, shooting moving targets, one handed unsupported shooting, shooting under cover). Students will develop shooting skills using pistols, revolvers, shotguns, carbines and rifles. The firearms training also includes a classroom instruction on weapons safety and the use of force.

 

Strategy and tactics training (STT - Crisis Management)

Because a sharp mind is our best weapon, the STT program focuses on mental preparedness. It provides courses to operate hostile or difficult environments, to identify, anticipate and reduce risks, to minimize firearm use.
The following courses are available:
• Professional conduct in the workplace
• The public interaction course
• Tactical communication course
• Performance enhancement course
• Cultural diversity
• The drug awareness program

 

Information gathering (IG)

The course is based on the process of collection and analysis of information using “closed sources “ and “open sources”: espionage, radio broadcasting, Internet search engines, newspapers, archive databases such as LexisNexis, public libraries, private investigators… The class also presents cryptanalytic and cryptography researches.

 

Law Enforcement Training (LET - certification program)

This program provides an up-to-date and relevant training to police forces who are charged with the responsibility of maintaining law and order.
The program addresses a variety of topics:
• Violations of laws
• Summary offences (infraction, misdemeanor…)
• Indictable offences (severe crimes)
• Maintenance of public order
• Summons and legal proceedings
• Citations and local magistrate

 

Driving training

SNST offers a range of driving courses to train students beyond mastery of basic mechanics of driving. The program addresses a range of techniques to deal with the worst eventualities: vehicle attacks, speed driving, off-road driving, emergency stops, slippery surfaces, crashes…

 

Cash In Transit training (CITT)

At SNST we train security officers to transfer currency or valuable goods safely and efficiently from one location to another. The course includes a 6-hour classroom instruction on the very latest security technology and international door-to-door transportation. Role plays are also integrated in the program : point-to-point cash in transit in both extremely hostile and more benign environments.

 

Self-defense (SD)

The course is designed to cover all aspects of defensive tactics: defending onself, one's property or one's home.
Students are taught positioning strategies and self-confidence for better self-defense. There is also a classroom instruction on how to remove oneself from a dangerous situation and avoid physical confrontations.
The program includes a class on surveillance and home security systems.
