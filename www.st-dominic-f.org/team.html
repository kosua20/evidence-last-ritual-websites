LEADERSHIP

The Managing Director supervises and directs the organization’s business affairs and administers financial, physical and human resources pursuant to its mission, statutory obligations and the decisions of the National Council. The Managing Director is chosen by the National Council.
The Managing Director puts policies, programs and activities before the National Council and implements the Council’s decisions. The Managing Director serves as the link between the National Council, the Executive Committee and the various departments; the Managing Director assures the implementation and follow-up of programs, hires personnel and represents the organization.
The Deputy Managing Director shares with the Managing Director responsibility for the smooth functioning of the organization. The Deputy Managing Director is also responsible for The Saint Dominic Foundation’s corporate communications.
Directors are responsible for the management of their respective departments. As members of the management team, they advise the Managing Director, participate in the planning of the organization’s programs, guarantee coordination of activities and evaluate the productivity of the employees in their department.

DEPARTMENTS

EDUCATION
The Education Department deals principally with two national campaigns launched each year.
  1. The Fall Action Campaign is an advocacy campaign aimed at promoting greater economic justice led in support of our partners in developing countries.
  2. Share Lent is The Saint Dominic Foundation’s great annual fundraising campaign, culminating in Solidarity Sunday. It also includes the Lent Youth-Fast.
The Education Department is made up of a Popular Education Team, a Fundraising Team and an Activities Team.

DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS
The Development Programs Department, in concert with our partners in developing countries, has as its mission to analyze the economic, social, political and cultural conditions that influence the lives of Third-World populations. It strives to implement programs that allow populations of developing nations to gain greater self-sufficiency and guarantee their long-term development.
The Development Programs Department brings together teams focused on Africa, Asia, Latin America and emergency services.

COMMUNICATIONS AND RESEARCH
The Communications and Research Department has as its mission to ensure that the media disseminate the message of solidarity and justice conveyed by The Saint Dominic Foundation, and works to increase the organization’s visibility in the United States and abroad. Moreover, this department collaborates closely with the Education Department in launching the Fall Action and Share Lent Campaigns. To do so, it has recourse to a wide range of resources and written and electronic communications programs. The Communications and Research Department is made up of communications and research specialists.

ADMINISTRATION
The Administrative Department is responsible for accounting and finance, computer equipment, work relations and overall administrative service delivery.
Besides personnel responsible for general administrative tasks, the Administrative Department is made up of accounting and computer science specialists.
